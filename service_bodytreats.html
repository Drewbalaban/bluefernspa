What to Expect from a Wrap

Your Complete Guide to Body Wrap Treatments

Before Your Treatment

You'll undress or change into the provided disposable underwear – in privacy. Our spa therapists are great at respecting their clients' modesty, so you will be covered for the duration of the session.

Skin Preparation

Next, you will be treated with a gentle dry exfoliation or scrub to slough away any dead skin cells. This prepares your skin to better absorb the therapeutic benefits of the wrap treatment.

Application Process

Once your skin has been prepped, your therapist will apply a therapeutic body mask to your torso, and your arms and legs. The content of the mask used depends on the treatment you have chosen.

The Wrap Experience

After applying the mask to most of your body, your therapist will swaddle you in a plastic sheet, and a thermal blanket, then leave you to rest for around 20 to 30 minutes. If you're having a detox or slimming wrap, the process might be a bit different.

During the Treatment

The heat of the wrap will relax your muscles, open your pores and encourage sweating. To keep you comfortable, you might have a cold compress placed gently on your forehead or you'll be offered a relaxing scalp massage while you're wrapped in your body cocoon.

Completion & Aftercare

When you're freed from your cocoon, the therapist will cleanse & scrub away any remaining wrap lotion with hot towels and then will massage gently a moisturizing body cream into your newly baby-soft skin.

Important Note

Please let the therapist know from the start if you suffer from claustrophobia.

In-Spa Treatment

Bioslimming is an hour treatment. We advise 1 or 2 Bioslimming Wrap/week. To achieve maximum results we recommend 10 sessions within 6-8 weeks, if this is combined with the use of the Bioslimming Trio at home between salon treatments optimum results can be achieved.
