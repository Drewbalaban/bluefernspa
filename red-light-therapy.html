What is Red Light Therapy?

Red Light Therapy (RLT), also known as photobiomodulation, is an innovative treatment that uses low-power lasers, light-emitting diodes, or other specialized light sources to emit therapeutic red light directly into the body. Scientific studies have demonstrated that different frequencies of light produce distinct biological effects. Although the process may feel subtle, these specific wavelengths of red light penetrate deep into cells and create positive, measurable changes throughout the body.

⚡

How Does Red Light Therapy Work?

Red Light Therapy harnesses low levels of red or near-infrared light to energize your cells and promote natural healing and vitality. The precise wavelength red light produced by our advanced Red Light Therapy machines is absorbed by the mitochondria in your body—the cellular powerhouses responsible for energy production, healing processes, and overall day-to-day health maintenance. This cellular activation enhances your body's natural healing mechanisms from within.

Are There Any Risks?

Red Light Therapy or Photobiomodulation uses very low levels of therapeutic heat that do not hurt or burn the skin. Unlike tanning booths, RLT won't expose your skin to damaging UV rays. Like all services and modalities at Bluefern Spa, Red Light Therapy is a safe and effective wellness tool when used consistently and under professional guidance.

🔬

Red Light Therapy vs. Infrared Sauna

While both offer health benefits, RLT differs significantly from infrared saunas in both mechanism and purpose. Infrared saunas use near-infrared or far-infrared light to heat the body, impacting health by increasing heart rate, promoting perspiration, and activating heat shock proteins. Infrared light from saunas is invisible and penetrates much deeper into the body with wavelengths at 3,000-15,000 nm.

Red Light Therapy is not designed to heat the body. Instead, it specifically targets mitochondrial function and ATP production, essentially providing cellular nutrition so your cells function at their optimal level. This targeted approach makes RLT ideal for healing, recovery, and anti-aging benefits.
